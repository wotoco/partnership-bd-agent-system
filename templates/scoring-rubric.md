# Scoring Rubric

> Used by `opportunity-scorer`. Adjust dimensions and weights to what you actually optimise for. Weights should sum to ~100 (red flags subtract).

| Dimension | Weight | What a high score looks like |
|-----------|--------|------------------------------|
| Strategic fit | 30 | directly advances a current goal |
| Effort vs. return | 25 | low/clear cost, real upside |
| My differentiation | 20 | I can do this better than alternatives |
| Relationship value | 15 | opens credible future doors |
| Red flags | -10 max | vague terms, scope creep, misaligned incentives |

## Thresholds
- **80+** pursue, tailored approach
- **60–79** pursue if capacity allows
- **<60** decline or park

## My non-negotiables (auto-decline if hit)
- <e.g. no budget and no strategic upside>
- <e.g. exclusivity with no commitment>
